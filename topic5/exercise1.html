Tourist Attractions in London

The London Eye

The London Eye, also known as the Milleniumn Wheel, is one of the most popular tourist attractions in the UK and can offer phenomenal views of the UK.

The Natural History Museum

London's Natural History Museum has a lot of items from around the world, with multiple fossils of dinosaurs, various rare artifacts of humanity's past, as well as specimen from various creatures alive and dead.
